Admission Process 2025

Home > Admission Process

How to Apply

1

Online Application

Fill the online application form available on our website. Provide accurate personal and academic details. Ensure all required fields are completed.

2

Document Submission

Upload scanned copies of required documents including mark sheets, ID proof, and photographs. All documents must be clear and legible.

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Transfer Certificate
  • ID Proof (Aadhar/Passport)
  • Recent Photographs
3

Entrance Examination

Appear for our entrance test or submit scores of national level exams like SAT, JEE, or NEET as applicable. The exam tests aptitude, reasoning, and subject knowledge.

4

Personal Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview to assess aptitude and interests. This helps us understand your goals and aspirations.

5

Admission Confirmation

Pay the admission fee and complete the enrollment process to secure your seat. Welcome to Excellence University!

Eligibility Criteria

Undergraduate Programs (B.Tech, B.Sc, BBA, etc.)

  • Minimum 60% marks in 10+2 from a recognized board
  • Valid score in entrance examination (JEE/SAT) or university entrance test
  • Age: Between 17-25 years as on July 1, 2025
  • Proficiency in English language

Postgraduate Programs (M.Tech, MBA, M.Sc, etc.)

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum 55% marks from a recognized university
  • Valid GATE/CAT/GRE score or university entrance test
  • Work experience (preferred for MBA - 2 years)
  • Statement of Purpose and Letters of Recommendation

Doctoral Programs (Ph.D.)

  • Master's degree with minimum 60% marks in relevant field
  • Research proposal in area of interest
  • Qualify in university entrance test and interview
  • Publications in peer-reviewed journals (preferred)
